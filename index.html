OUR STORY

Today I pick up someone I'll get used to!

Mornings at Lemongrass usually start at 6:30 am. The first person to come to Lemongrass is always the cleaning lady. Normally crowded with guests like that, but the mornings in Sa is so quiet: you can clearly hear the sound of the ceiling fan "breathing" softly overhead. Her job will always be to fill the burners with essential oils. Light scented candles. Clean the floor. Clean up old towels and replace them all with brand new, white, fragrant towels. Then I will change the vase. Open the doors, sweep the yard, while outside, the birds don't stop chirping. A new day of Lemongrass is always like that.
