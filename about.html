ABOUT STRØM

THE ORIGIN OF THE WORD STRØM

Evoking the power and the tranquility of water, the term strøm, borrowed from Scandinavian languages, designates a current, but also means, figuratively, the energy linked to movement, to displacement. The term strøm illustrates the living force, channeled, which carries with it the vestiges of its source to nourish and deeply transform the course of things; it constitutes regeneration, towards a state of equilibrium.

HISTORY

The Strøm was founded from a simple idea: to combine Nordic spa and urbanity, for a rejuvenating experience of relaxation. This balance, which our customers also find, is based on facilities in the heart of nature, just minutes from urban centers. Officially open since 2009, Strøm continues to develop, both in terms of its growth and its public and tourist recognition.

MISSION

We aspire to be the equilibrium environment of a person and his community.

SELECTION

The Strøm Nordic spa contacted the non-profit organization L'APPUI, in order to be helped in the support and development of its project. APPUI allows caregivers of seniors to benefit from personalized support throughout their journey, during which an advisor will help them define their network and their needs, learn about the services available to them in their region or to establish those who would be useful to them, to recognize their aptitudes and their strengths, their difficulties and their limitations. Thanks to this conscientious support and knowledge of the family caregiver organization, Strøm can offer free services tailored to the needs of caregivers: access to the thermal experience, skin care, massage therapy, meditation, rest room. , access for people with reduced mobility, meals and snacks, etc. The offer is therefore adapted for each family caregiver so that they can derive the greatest possible benefit from it. The collaboration between L'APPUI and Strøm allows support even before the first appointment call at Strøm. Thus, the organization validates their interests and needs with candidates, and Strøm then takes care of the first contact with the caregiver. Strøm wishes to avoid any additional hassle and stress that may be associated with a new experience. To do this, a Strøm employee is completely dedicated to caregivers. He serves as the point of contact for all questions before, during and after the stay. He will also validate the needs of the caregiver and offer him activities and services adapted to his needs.
